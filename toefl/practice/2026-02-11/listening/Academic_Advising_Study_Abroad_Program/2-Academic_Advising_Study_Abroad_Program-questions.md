## Questions

**1. Why does the student visit the academic advisor?**
- A) To change her major to Environmental Science
- B) To get information about studying abroad in Costa Rica
- C) To discuss her graduation requirements
- D) To apply for a scholarship

**2. What is the student's main concern about studying abroad?**
- A) Whether it will interfere with her graduation timeline
- B) Whether she can learn Spanish effectively
- C) Whether the program will be safe
- D) Whether her parents will approve

**3. According to the advisor, what is an advantage of the OTS program?**
- A) It is taught entirely in Spanish
- B) Courses can fulfill upper-level Environmental Science requirements
- C) It is less expensive than other programs
- D) It includes a free summer internship

**4. Why does the advisor suggest the student might consider the spring semester instead of fall?**
- A) The spring weather is better for research
- B) The spring program is less expensive
- C) It would allow her to do the EPA internship in summer and complete coursework in fall
- D) More scholarships are available for spring programs

**5. What does the student say about the intersession program?**
- A) She has already applied for it
- B) She thinks it would not be valuable
- C) She is concerned it seems more manageable financially but might not be as enriching
- D) She prefers it to the full semester program

**6. According to the conversation, what are TWO requirements for applying for study abroad scholarships? (Choose 2 answers)**
- A) Submitting a statement of purpose
- B) Having a 3.5 GPA or higher
- C) Getting a faculty recommendation
- D) Completing an interview
- E) Demonstrating proficiency in Spanish

**7. What does the advisor say about housing in the OTS program?**
- A) Students live only with host families
- B) Students stay in hotels near research sites
- C) Students live in dormitories at research stations and stay with host families during breaks
- D) Students must arrange their own housing

**8. Listen again to part of the conversation. Then answer the question.**

*"Hmm, but wouldn't spring semester be during the dry season in Costa Rica? I thought the field research was better during the wet season."*

**What does the student imply when she says this?**
- A) She prefers to travel during dry weather
- B) She has researched the optimal time for environmental fieldwork in Costa Rica
- C) She wants to avoid the wet season
- D) She disagrees with the advisor's recommendation
